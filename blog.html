My first week in Madison May 18-25, 2018

I arrived in Madison Wisconsin at my new home away from home late in the evening on May 18. I spent that first weekend getting situated in the house and getting familiar with my neighboorhood, campus, and around the capitol. Madison is so lively and the lake (which is two blocks away from the house) is beautiful. The first day in the lab I met all the graduate students, post-docs, and professors in Professor Austerweil's lab and some of us went out to lunch together. The rest of the week in the lab was spent getting my computer fixed at IT, downloading software, and reading papers for my lit review. Overall I think it has been a good first week, and next week I should get more familiar with my project and get connected to the lab server.
